How can I visit the Googleplex?

You can drive to Google's main campus. You are welcome to wonder around the outside of the campus, take pictures of the various statues, and take pictures in front of the buildings. But if you want to enter the building, and take a proper tour, you will need a Googler to escort you. So your best approach is to befriend some Googlers.

Xin Li, Staff Software Engineer at Google Answer on Quora
